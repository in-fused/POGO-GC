What this map shows

🌧️ Real Weather
LIVE
Current weather via OpenWeatherMap → maps to PoGo's 7 in-game weather conditions → shows exactly which types are boosted right now. Boosted Pokemon spawn more, are higher level (up to L35 wild), and gain +20% damage.
🌳 Park & Nature Zones
REAL OSM DATA
Green overlays are real parks, forests, and nature reserves from OpenStreetMap. In PoGo these are the primary locations for Grass, Bug, and Normal type spawns — and are where nests appear.
💧 Water Bodies
REAL OSM DATA
Blue overlays are real rivers, lakes, and coastline from OpenStreetMap. Water, Ice, and occasionally Electric types concentrate near these zones in PoGo.
⚔️ Current Raid Bosses
LIVE
Fetched live from pogoapi.net — the actual current T1 through T5 raid bosses in rotation. Tap the sword button on the map to see the full board.
📍 Simulated Spawn Pins
REMOVED
Removed. Fake spawn pins next to the real game created confusion — Pokemon shown in the app weren't actually there. Only real, verifiable data is displayed.

What we can't show (and why)

Real-time spawn locations
UNAVAILABLE
Niantic has no public API. All past real-time trackers (PokéVision etc.) used reverse-engineered calls and were shut down. Self-hosted scanners exist but require bot accounts and violate ToS.
IV / CP / exact despawn time
UNAVAILABLE
Only accessible via ToS-violating scanners. Not something this app will use.
The strategy: use the biome zones and weather boost to decide where to walk and what to expect — then use chat to coordinate raids with your group in real time.
